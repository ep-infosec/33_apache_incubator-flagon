The Apache Flagon team happily welcomes contributors and supporters of our work. Contributions, bug reports, and suggestions are welcome for the core products, website, documentation, and everything else! See our docs for more information on submitting effective contributions and bug reports.

Mailing Lists

Users

For users of Apache Flagon who want to keep up to date with new releases and community highlights. To subscribe, email user-subscribe@flagon.incubator.apache.org. User List

Dev

For contributors to Apache Flagon or those interested in ongoing design and development efforts. To subscribe, email dev-subscribe@flagon.incubator.apache.org. Dev List

Commits

For emails-on-commit across all Apache Flagon products. To subscribe, email commits-subscribe@flagon.incubator.apache.org. Commits List

Community Hubs

GitHub

Find our code on Github to browse and contribute.

Twitter

Follow us on Twitter to keep up to date on Apache Flagon and related developments.

StackOverflow

Get your questions answered on StackOverflow.

Issues and Bugs

Visit our JIRA Issue Tracker for full information on current developments, bugs, etc.

Wiki

See our wiki for detailed information on project organization, roadmaps, etc.
